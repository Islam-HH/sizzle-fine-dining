The Story of Sizzle

About Restaurant

At Sizzle, we believe that fine dining is an art form. Our chefs combine fresh, locally sourced ingredients with culinary expertise to create dishes that delight the senses. Whether you are celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a night out, our elegant atmosphere and attentive service ensure a memorable experience.

Founded in 1985 by the visionary Chef Ramadan El Sokkary, Sizzle started as a quaint bistro nestled in the heart of Paris. Chef El Sokkary dreamed of a place where traditional French cooking could meet flavors from around the world. The name 'Sizzle' was born from the exciting sound of his signature dishes being crafted on a searing hot grill. Through the years, Sizzle has transformed from a cherished local spot to a world-renowned fine dining destination, celebrated for its creativity, quality, and dedication to the art of cuisine. Our story is one of passion, and we are delighted to share it with every guest who walks through our doors.
